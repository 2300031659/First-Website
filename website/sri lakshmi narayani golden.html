Temple structure

The entire temple is covered with 1,500 kg gold plates. The Sri Lakshmi Narayani shrine Vimanam and Artha Mandapam in this temple are covered with pure gold plates. . The temple is spread over 100 acres of beautiful gardens in the constellation of Sri Chakram. The temple was built by Narayani Peedam, a foundation based in Vellore.
